What is China College Students' Voice Survey?

China Collage Students' Voice Survey (CCSVS) is a student-oriented questionnaire system designed to understand and serve Students. Questionnaire completed by college students study associations alliance to jointly develop, the growth and development of the national college students' group in the "voice" investigation, focus on new era college students generally concerned about the hot issues, for students to view the first-hand information, provide reliable scientific information source for academic research, for students to accurately understand and effective development of self help and guidance, It provides data support for government and other public departments to make decisions.

Now our questionnaires and database are open to scholars in various fields. If you are interested in our investigations, welcome to contact us at : dianshi2000@126.com; lid.sxy@gmail.com
